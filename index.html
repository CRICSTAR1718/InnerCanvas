💡 Productivity Tips

📝 Task Management

  • Break large tasks into smaller, manageable pieces
  • Use the priority system to focus on what matters most
  • Review and update your tasks daily
  • Celebrate completing tasks to stay motivated

😊 Mood Tracking

  • Log your mood at the same time each day for consistency
  • Add notes to understand what influences your mood
  • Review your mood patterns weekly to identify trends
  • Use the calendar view to see your emotional journey

📖 Journaling

  • Write regularly, even if just a few sentences
  • Use different themes to match your mood
  • Include details about your day and feelings
  • Don't worry about perfect grammar - focus on expression
